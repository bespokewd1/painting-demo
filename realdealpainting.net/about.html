Who We Are

Real Deal Painting: NW Indiana's Trusted Painting Contractors

Our pride lies in delivering high-quality work. Every member of the Real Deal Painting team is directly employed by the company, ensuring fair wages and full benefits. We believe that a well-cared-for team is integral to providing exceptional services. Established by a seasoned painter, Real Deal Painting recognizes the artistry and skill required in painting services. Our experts engage in thorough surface preparation, pressure washing, and use of high-quality materials to ensure a durable and polished finish.

As a locally owned and operated company, we are deeply rooted in the community, and our commitment extends beyond just painting; it is about building relationships and contributing to the beauty of our neighborhood. Your vision, combined with our expertise, creates the real deal. Get in touch with us for a free quote, and let us redefine the aesthetics of your space.
