Selecting Colors with Real Deal Painting

When it comes to exterior painting, it's vital to take into account the established colors of your home, such as brick, stonework, and roofing. The ideal color schemes are those that complement these existing features.

Consider embracing a shade that enhances the color of an unpainted section - like highlighting the brown in your brick or a shade of green from your stonework.

It's also essential to remember that your home is part of a community; its aesthetic is influenced by neighboring homes and the surrounding landscape. While you may not want your house to be the exact color as your neighbor's, selecting a shade that harmonizes with nearby homes can be beneficial.

Additionally, be mindful that certain colors are more suited to specific environments. For instance, earth tones are typically a great choice for natural, wooded areas.

Quality Paint Options with Real Deal Painting

The external factors such as extreme temperatures, humidity, and continuous sun exposure can deteriorate the exterior paint of our homes. When paint breaks down, it can expose cracks which allow damaging moisture to enter the home. Quality paint can protect against severe weather while allowing damaging moisture to escape.

Nowadays, there are numerous options for weather-resistant paints.

Elastomeric coatings are gaining popularity; these coatings maintain their elasticity across a broad temperature range, making them ideal for homes in areas with extreme weather conditions.

Real Deal Painting’s Exterior Painting Process

Surface Preparation

Proper surface preparation is fundamental to any painting project. Various methods can be employed, and it's crucial to determine what preparation is needed to achieve the desired results. Some preparation techniques are designed for long-lasting results, while others are aimed at enhancing the appearance.

Pressure Washing

The first step in ensuring a high-quality finish is a clean surface. If there is any mold, we will hand wash the area with an appropriate cleaning solution. Pressure washing the exterior removes mold, dirt, and old paint, providing a solid base for the new paint to adhere to.

Pressure washing can also be used to clean exterior driveways, sidewalks, and stone walls by removing grease, dirt, and moss. As pressure washers are powerful machines, only trained professionals will handle this equipment.

Use of Drop Cloths

Real Deal Painting uses heavy-duty canvas drop cloths. We place them around the home where scraping will occur. This practice protects your plants, pavement, and soil, and enables easier cleanup at the end of the day. The result is a spotless environment once the job is complete.

Caulking

Caulking is a pliable sealant used in gaps where expansion and contraction are expected, such as around windows and doors. It prevents moisture from entering the wood, which is often the cause of paint failure around these areas.

Only paintable latex caulking is used on your home. Silicone caulking is not paintable and must be removed and replaced before painting as it is typically used for bathrooms.
